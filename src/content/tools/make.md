longDescription: "Make (formerly Integromat) is a visual workflow automation platform that connects over 1,800 apps and services. Where Zapier uses a linear trigger-action model, Make uses a visual canvas where you build automation scenarios by dragging and connecting modules — making it significantly more powerful for complex, multi-step workflows.\n\nThe visual builder is Make's defining advantage. You can see the entire flow of your automation at a glance, with branching paths, filters, error handlers, and iterators clearly mapped out. For automations that involve conditional logic — 'if this, do A; if not, do B' — Make is far more intuitive than text-based alternatives.\n\nMake recently introduced AI features including an AI assistant that helps build scenarios from natural language descriptions, AI-powered error resolution, and integrations with AI services like OpenAI, Claude, and Stable Diffusion. You can build automations that generate content, process images, analyze data, or interact with AI models as part of larger workflows.\n\nThe free plan includes 1,000 operations per month — enough for light automation like syncing data between two apps or sending notifications. The pricing model is based on operations (individual steps in a scenario), which can be more cost-effective than Zapier for complex multi-step automations but harder to predict for simple ones."
