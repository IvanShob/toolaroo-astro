With continuous crawling of over 10 billion web pages and a link index that updates faster than most competitors, Ahrefs gives digital marketers and SEO agencies unparalleled visibility into the link profiles of any website — their own or their competitors.\n\nThe platform's Site Explorer is its flagship tool, providing a comprehensive breakdown of any website's organic search performance, including estimated organic traffic, top-performing pages, organic keyword rankings, and complete backlink profile. Competitors' pages can be analyzed in the same depth, revealing exactly which content earns the most backlinks and traffic — intelligence that informs both content strategy and link building campaigns. The content gap analysis surfaces keywords that competitors rank for but a given site does not, identifying high-value content opportunities.\n\nAhrefs' Keywords Explorer is one of the most reliable keyword research tools in the industry, providing accurate search volume data, keyword difficulty scores, click-through rate estimates, and SERP analysis across multiple search engines including Google, Bing, YouTube, and Amazon. The keyword difficulty metric accounts for the realistic number of backlinks needed to rank on the first page — giving content teams a practical measure of the investment required to compete for any given term.\n\nThe Site Audit tool conducts automated technical SEO crawls, identifying issues like broken links, redirect chains, missing meta data, duplicate content, and Core Web Vitals problems across the entire site. The Rank Tracker monitors keyword position changes over time across multiple countries and devices, with email alerts for significant ranking movements. For content teams, the Content Explorer searches the web for high-performing content in any niche, showing which articles have earned the most backlinks and shares — essential research for planning link-worthy content."
