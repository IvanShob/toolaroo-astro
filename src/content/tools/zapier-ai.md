longDescription: "Zapier is the most widely used workflow automation platform, connecting over 7,000 apps so they can talk to each other without code. When something happens in one app (a new email, a form submission, a Shopify order), Zapier can automatically trigger actions in other apps — sending a Slack message, updating a spreadsheet, creating a task, or any combination of steps.\n\nThe AI features in Zapier make building automations dramatically easier. Instead of manually configuring triggers and actions, you can describe what you want in plain English — 'When someone fills out my Typeform, add them to my Mailchimp list and send a Slack notification to the sales channel' — and Zapier's AI builds the workflow for you. It suggests the right apps, maps the fields, and configures the logic.\n\nZapier also includes AI-powered actions within workflows. You can add a step that uses AI to summarize text, extract data from emails, classify support tickets, draft responses, or transform data between formats. These AI steps run inside your existing automations, meaning you can add intelligence to any workflow without building separate AI integrations.\n\nThe platform scales from simple two-app connections to complex multi-step workflows with conditional logic, filters, delays, and error handling. For teams, Zapier offers shared workspaces where automations are owned by the organization rather than individual accounts. The free plan includes 100 tasks/month across 5 single-step automations — enough to test the concept, but most active users will need a paid plan."
