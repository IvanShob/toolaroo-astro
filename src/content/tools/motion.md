longDescription: "Motion is an AI-powered productivity platform that solves one of the most common challenges in modern knowledge work: figuring out what to work on when. Motion eliminates this gap by treating tasks with the same calendar-level seriousness as meetings. When you add a task with a deadline and estimated time, Motion automatically finds a slot in your calendar and books it. If a new meeting is added, Motion reschedules affected tasks intelligently rather than leaving them unplanned.\n\nMotion's AI scheduler continuously replans the day in real time. When tasks take longer than expected, meetings run over, or new urgent items are added, Motion recalculates the entire schedule to accommodate changes — surfacing what must be deferred, flagging deadlines at risk, and ensuring nothing critical falls through the cracks. This dynamic rescheduling is what most manual time-blocking systems fail at: the ability to adapt gracefully when reality diverges from the plan.\n\nFor teams, Motion includes a project management layer that combines task management, project timelines, and individual scheduling into a unified system. Managers can assign tasks to team members with deadlines, and Motion will automatically schedule those tasks into each person's calendar based on their individual availability and workload. This removes the need for manual sprint planning meetings simply to figure out who has capacity for what. With integrations for Google Calendar, Outlook, Zoom, and project management tools, Motion positions itself as a comprehensive work management hub for high-output individuals and teams."
