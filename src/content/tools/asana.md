Marketing teams often use the Calendar view to manage editorial calendars alongside campaign timelines.\n\nAsana's rule-based automation allows teams to build triggers without code — such as 'when a task is marked complete, notify the client contact' or 'when a subtask is added, assign it to the QA team.' These automations reduce manual update work and keep workflows moving without requiring active supervision. Combined with over 200 native integrations including Slack, GitHub, Google Workspace, Zoom, and Salesforce, Asana plugs naturally into existing enterprise tool stacks."
